Project LOVE gathering

Project LOVE aims to contribute in the building of more inclusive online communities by increasing awareness, advancing knowledge, building capacity, and supporting decision making. We are meeting from the 25 to the 28 of November to co-designw a community-driven ethical framework for collecting, analysing, and sharing social networks and online communities data. Four days of split-and-reconvene sessions, dialogues, and social interactions.

Credit goes to rOpenSci for the structure of this type of event. We have a strict code of conduct and terms and conditions.

All FIXME ideas will begin as GitHub issues on the FIXME repo in the weeks before the event.

Who’s coming?

We are promoting the participation of a wide representation of our communities. The event is open to everybody: current partners, prospective partners, external members, interested contributors, and everybody who is working to nurture more inclusive online communities.
